Principal – Sri Ramadevi

Sri Ramadevi is the respected principal of Shri Gnanambica Degree College. She has more than twenty years of experience in the field of education and academic leadership. Under her guidance, the college has achieved good academic standards and discipline among students. She always encourages students to focus on knowledge, skills, and personal development. Her vision is to create an environment where students can learn with confidence and achieve success in their careers.

Correspondent – R. Garu Prasad

R. Garu Prasad is the correspondent of Shri Gnanambica Degree College and plays an important role in the development of the institution. He is committed to providing quality education and modern facilities for students. His vision is to make the college a center of excellence in higher education.
